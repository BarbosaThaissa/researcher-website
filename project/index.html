Thank you for visiting! I’m a geoscientist with an enduring fascination for understanding the dynamic processes that shape our planet — and other celestial bodies. My quest? To uncover how both natural and human-induced surface processes sculpt the landscapes we see today.
Through this website, my goal is to share and expand the scientific knowledge I generate, striving to make it more accessible and inspiring for everyone

My Research Focus

Fluvial
and
Landforms
I am to improve our understanding of the processes and factors that led to the evolução da planicies aluviais ao longo do quaternario tardio e os impactos recentes de mudanças do clima e induzidas antropicamente.
Aeolian
and
Landforms
My primary focus in this topic has been on aeolian records (aeolian fields and loess) in Northeast Brazil, mainly the aeolian dunefield of the middle São Francisco.
Coastal
and
Submarine
My research focus has been on the coastal and submarine areas of the Sergipe-Alagoas basin, Northeast Brazil, aiming to study the interactions between river-ocean incluindo as respostas a variação do nível do mar over do quaternario tardio.
